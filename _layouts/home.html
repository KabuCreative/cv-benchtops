What do we do?

We are central Victoria's leading manufacturer of benchtops and supplier of kitchen hardware. We work with local cabinet makers to deliver quality kitchens to suit any style or budget.

How can we help you?

We can help you plan and source your new kitchen. Visit our showroom to discover our extensive range of materials and fittings - you'll be surprised at what options are available.

What areas do we service?

As our name suggests, we service all of central Victoria, including Bendigo, Mildura, Shepparton, Kyneton, Maryborough, Castlemaine and Swan Hill. Our showroom is located in Bendigo.

Why Choose us?

Since 1989 we've built our business on quality - in our manufacturing, the products we supply, and in our customer support. Contact us to find out how we can help you.
